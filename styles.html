Styles

Versatility has always been a hallmark of Jeff Sanders' guitar and bass playing. Somehow, Jeff manages to sound completely at home in almost any type of music, from rock-n-roll to show tunes to R&B to classical.

Original Rock - Founding member of the Sharkbites, a popular original rock band in northeast Ohio in the late 1980s and early 1990s.
 
Classic and Oldies Rock - Played with classic acts such as the Coasters and the Diamonds
  Lead and rhythm guitar for many pop and classic rock cover bands
 
Big Band & Show Tunes - Musical director and stage performer for the widely acclaimed Easy Street theater company
  Played with the Lawrence Welk orchestra
  Played with the Al Martino orchestra
 
Country & Western - Frequently appeared with casino favorite TJ Weaver
 
Classical - Studied classical guitar at the well-respected Dana School of Music (Youngstown State University)
  Played with the Youngstown Symphony Orchestra
 
Rhythm & Blues - Frequently appeared with classic soul band Steppin Stonz
